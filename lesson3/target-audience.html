Target Audience

The target audience for the Logan City Chamber of Commerce website will primarily be business owners. From this group, there will be a wide range when it comes to size of businesses. Besides business owners, there will likely be executive staff representing their business, though they might not be considered its "owner". These visitors will have varying education levels, but will most likely have some post-secondary education experience. Their ages will range from 25 to 60.
